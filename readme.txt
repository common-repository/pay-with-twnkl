Pay With TWNKL enables customers to pay with crypto coins on your WooCommerce store.

== Description ==

Pay With TWNKL enables customers to pay with Twinkle coins on your WooCommerce store. Your customers will be offered the option of paying with the coin at checkout. If they choose that option then they will be quoted a price of that coin for their order automatically.

After submitting their order they will be given the details of the transaction they should make.

Features:

* Automatically convert order value to the coin at checkout
* Option for adding a percentage mark-up to the converted price
* Option for adding a percentage mark-down to the converted price
* Integration with [pernumpay.com](https://pernumpay.com) for automatic transaction tracking / reconciliation and order updates

Rainbow Currency:

For more information on the coin itself, see [twinklecoins.com](https://twinklecoins.com)

== Installation ==

* Install it as you would any other plugin
* Activate it
* Head over to WooCommerce » Settings » Checkout » Pay With TWNKL
* Enter your Business PerNum to receive payments, confirm markup % and you're ready to go.
